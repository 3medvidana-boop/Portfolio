How I Guide Complex Products from Idea to Launch. My Collaborative Design Process

Workflow

1

Context & Insight Alignment

I immerse myself in your product's context to uncover key user needs and competitor insights, aligning goals and priorities to create a clear foundation for informed, evidence-driven design decisions

2

Roadmap & Structure

Next, I produce a clear architecture: how features connect, what technical boundaries exist, and where milestones fall. I share this plan with you—not as a guarantee of perfection, but as our practical reference point. Together, we define priorities and get ready for the unpredictable moments every big project brings.

3

Prototyping Together & Iterative Refinement

I create wireframes and interactive prototypes that invite collaboration and critique, then refine the final UI through reusable design systems, accessibility checks, and continuous feedback—ensuring every component is tested, approved, and adaptable without delays or last-minute changes.

4

Concept Review & Risk Check

I craft wireframes and interactive demos. These are meant to be challenged and refined: we walk through scenarios, usability tests, and adapt the flow. I expect questions, and active decision-making from your side. This is the groundwork for robust, user-centric foundations.

5

Handoff, Prep & Support

I transfer assets with usage guidelines, specs, and direct support for your development team. I stay engaged throughout implementation—helping to ensure the design intent survives development and real-world issues are solved quickly.
